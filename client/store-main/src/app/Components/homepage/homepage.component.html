About Us

Visionlife Human Rights Foundation (VHRF) is a fully Non profit organisation, started on 7 October 2020. The Organization is registered in section 8 under the company act 2013. The NGO is recognized by NITI Aayog, Government of India. The registration No. this organization under NITI Aayog is WB/2020/0267206. Its main objective is to spread awareness of human rights of law in India. It is a NGO with a clear mission to protect and promote Human Rights and Fight against Child Labour, Women Rights etc.

Our Mission

Visionlife Human Rights Foundation is to try to bring awareness for the welfare of Human Beings in the society and to create an atmosphere of harmony without cost, Religion, classless, High and low in the society, and whatever work the organization has to do for human welfare, it will do.

Our Vision

The main vision of this organization is to try to reach our services to the common man and establishing the office of the organization across the country to do all the work related to human welfare, Human peace, Human harmony, Human unity and protection of human rights and to provide cooperation to the government and administration in eradicating corruption.

Objective

Our main objective is to provide complete information about human rights law to every person of the society and to spread awareness of the fundamental rights given in part 3 of the Indian Constitution keeping in touch with the national human rights Commission and follow all the guideline of NHRC.
