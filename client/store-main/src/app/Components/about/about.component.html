About Visionlife Human Rights Foundation (VHRF)

  • Established on October 7, 2020, registered under section 8 of the Companies Act, 2013.
  • Recognized by NITI Aayog, Government of India, with registration number WB/2020/0267206.
  • Dedicated to raising awareness about human rights laws in India.
  • Mission is to protect and promote human rights, combat issues such as child labor and women's rights violations.

Our Mission

  • Create a society where human welfare knows no boundaries of caste, religion, or social status.
  • Foster harmony and unity while safeguarding human rights.
  • Work hand in hand with government authorities to eliminate corruption and promote a culture of respect for fundamental human rights.

Our Vision

  • Extend services nationwide to serve the cause of human welfare, peace, harmony, and unity.
  • Collaborate closely with governmental bodies to eradicate corruption and uphold human rights at all levels of society.

Objectives

  • Educate every individual about their human rights as enshrined in the Indian Constitution, particularly those outlined in Part III.
  • Remain in constant communication with the National Human Rights Commission (NHRC), adhering to their guidelines while spreading awareness about fundamental rights.

Chairman’s Message

  • Human rights stem from innate humanity, evolving with us from birth to death.
  • Prioritize social justice over divisive politics.
  • Empower individuals to assert their rights peacefully and justly, fostering a society free from corruption and crime for future generations.

Thank you,

Kuldip Kumar Singh

National President, VHRF
